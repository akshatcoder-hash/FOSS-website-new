FOSSCU and Open Source

From the earliest days of computers, programmers and engineers developed new technologies through collaboration. The knowledge is shared, and the entire community benefits from the collective innovation. FOSSCU (Free and Open Source Software Community United) adopts these notions and thus focuses on promoting Development Culture among the students and acquainting them with the power of Open Source. To do the same, we provide mentorship to the members. We always put our best confidence in Collaboration over Competition.


Events at FOSSCU

FOSSCU organizes various boot camps and MSoC (Monsoon Summer of Code) which is an online program primarily focused on bringing in new contributions and giving students a real-world experience of events like GSoC or GSSoC.


Diversity in FOSSCU

FOSSCU supports all diverse groups and Women In Tech which is dedicated to help close the gender gap and to help women embrace technology and make them feel welcomed in the community.
